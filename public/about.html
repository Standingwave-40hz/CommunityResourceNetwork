About CommShare

CommShare is a community resource sharing platform designed to support local exchange of tools, food, crafts, and services. Whether you're a gardener with extra produce, a craftsperson with surplus materials, or a neighbor in need of a tool for the weekend, CommShare helps bring people together to share what they have.

The platform allows users to:

  • Post listings for items available to share or trade
  • Browse and search local listings by category or keyword
  • Manage their own tool and item listings
  • Track borrow/return history for community accountability
